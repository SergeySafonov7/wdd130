Moscow Oblast

Moscow Oblast is a region located in western Russia, surrounding the capital city of Moscow. It shares borders with Tver Oblast to the northwest, Ivanovo Oblast to the northeast, Vladimir Oblast to the east, and Kaluga and Bryansk Oblasts to the south. The region serves as a hub for transportation, industry, and agriculture, playing a crucial role in the economy of the Moscow metropolitan area. Notably, Moscow Oblast is known for its rich cultural heritage, featuring numerous historical sites, parks, and nature reserves, making it a popular destination for both residents and tourists seeking outdoor activities and cultural experiences. The administrative center of the region is the city of Krasnogorsk.
